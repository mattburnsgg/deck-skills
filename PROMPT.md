# The prompt

Paste this to your agent to kick off a deck.

```
Build me a slide deck about [your topic].

First, use prep-the-deck. Grill me relentlessly, one question at a time, with a
recommended answer each time, until the brief is locked. Do not start building
until the brief is locked.

Then use build-the-deck to produce a single-file HTML deck that runs in any
browser, plus a PDF.

Hard rules: never invent a chart or a graph. Use only real data you can capture
from a source, or a big number with a citation. Every slide has to be readable
from the back of a room.
```

That is the whole interface. The skills do the rest.
